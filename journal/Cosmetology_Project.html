Cosmetology Project Journal

The purpose of my form is to allow people to make reservations for an appointment in the Cosmetology room

In my form I used the input types:

  • Email
  • Text with a partern to make it they had to type a phone number
  • Time with a min time of 8:00 am and a max time of 4:00 pm

The request method I used is POST.

During the devolpment process of this project I didn't have any problems with pulling, merging, or the branch-based development, apart from when we had some problems because we forgot to make a .gitignore file and DS_Store caused some bugs.
